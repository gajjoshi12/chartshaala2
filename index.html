Frequently Asked

Answers before you ask.

Straight talk on brokerage, payouts, and how the learning platform works.

Is ChartShaala really zero brokerage?+
Yes — we charge zero brokerage on equity delivery, intraday, and F&O. Statutory charges (STT, exchange fees, GST, stamp duty) apply as mandated by SEBI and exchanges; those aren't ours to waive.
How long does onboarding take?+
Under a minute for demat-linked signup with PAN + Aadhaar eKYC. Funding via UPI is instant; NEFT settles in ~30 minutes on business days.
Do I get one-on-one mentor time?+
Every paid tier includes mentor hours. The Pro tier adds a weekly 1:1 review of your trade journal, position sizing, and risk metrics — the part that actually compounds.
Can I practice without real money?+
Yes. Our paper-trading simulator uses live market data, real spreads, and realistic slippage. You build a track record inside ChartShaala before risking a single rupee.
Are withdrawals really instant?+
UPI & IMPS withdrawals are processed in under a minute during banking hours. Bank-transfer withdrawals land within 24 hours on business days.
Do you guarantee returns?+
No — and nobody honest does. Trading involves risk. We teach frameworks, risk management, and discipline. Your results depend on you, your capital, and market conditions.
